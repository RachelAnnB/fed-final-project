About

A Modern Eden is a line of products designed for style savvy parents who care about what they put in their kids’ rooms, prints, totes, accessories, and mobile apps that feature mid-century modern animal illustrations. Developed by creative agency Sparkbox, located in Dayton, Ohio, A Modern Eden products feature sustainable materials and hand-crafted goodness.
